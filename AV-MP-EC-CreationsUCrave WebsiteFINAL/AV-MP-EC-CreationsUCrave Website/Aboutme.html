About Us

Creations U Crave is a Pop-Up Shop located in the East Bay Area that serves home-made baked goods and Central American foods.

We host our pop-ups in areas such as Castro Valley, advertise our menus on social media and allow customers to pre-order online and pick-up on the day of. At Creations U Crave, we hope to welcome and bring comfort to our customers through our foods, “from our home to yours.”
